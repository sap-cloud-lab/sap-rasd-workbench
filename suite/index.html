2608 Release Readiness

2608 is the readiness anchor for test, development and production planning.

  • APJ release planning and preliminary What's New review are tracked through Public Maintenance Watch.
  • RASD impact cards focus the 2608 review on used scope, IAM successor apps and Cloud ALM test packs.
  • Priority actions include predecessor app usage checks, role activation and cutover readiness.

AI Use Cases

AI opportunities sit inside the same release and clean-core governance rhythm.
